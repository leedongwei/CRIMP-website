We've setup a 4 day event, with 23 categories and 800 climbers in 1 hour

  • Setup Event 2-3mins
    • Pick a custom URL for your webpage
    • Enter event info and dates
  • Setup Categories and Routes 2-3min/category
    • Enter category info and date/time
    • Setup route-specific info
  • Upload Climbers to Category 3-5min/category
    • Bulk upload climbers using Excel files
    • We'll generate individualized score-chits for you
  • Add your crew 10mins
    • Invite your Chief Judge to the platform
    • Text/email our app download link to your judges
    • Judges download our Android app
    • Approve their access to your event
    • Brief judges on usage of app (slides provided)
  • Before each Category Event Day
    • Give out score-chits to climbers at isolation
    • Chief Judge checks every Judge is at the correct route through our dashboard
  • During each Category Event Day
    • Judges submit scores through CRIMP app
    • Chief Judge monitors event through our dashboard
  • After each Category Event Day
    • Chief Judge debrief Judges
    • Mark scores as confirmed
    • We'll take the final results to generate an Excel file for you to use
