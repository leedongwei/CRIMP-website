It's time to ditch Excel

We reinvent scoring to supercharge the spectating experience at your climbing event Deliver real-time scores to your climbers while simplifying scorekeeping for your crew

Scorekeeping for the 21st century climbing event

View your scores online

Everyone can see the scores from their phones. Now all our friends can know that a 12 year old kid flashed a climb that we can't even start on.

> See the scoreboard at demo.crimp.rocks

Live score updates

Let your climbers track their progress as the event happens! New scores are pushed to their phones instantly. Waiting 3 hours for manual score tabulation is a thing of the past.

> Live scores can be turned off

See who's climbing on the wall

The app tracks the judges and climbers active on the wall. And we share that on the scoreboard so we can finally learn and spell the names of our favourite Russian climber.

Easy-to-use app for route judges

Scores are submitted and tabulated at the push of a button. Bad reception? The app elegantly handles that without human intervention. We let the judges focus on catching foot dabs and no-match-finishes.

Multiple scoring systems are supported

If you plan to use a score system that we do not have, contact us 1 month in advance and we'll work with you to add it in!

Individualized score-chits for your climbers

We'll generate them in a PDF that is ready-for-printing. Designed for use with our scoring app to speed up scoring. Collect them after the climbs and they'll double as a hard-copy backup!

Live-data for live-broadcasts

Our servers know everything about the climbers on your wall. Text-overlay on video streams can be so much richer with our support.

Tell someone about us!

We are new and we could really use your help to reach out to people who'll find us useful.

If you know folks that organize climbing events at any level, please let them know we exist!
